Security & Privacy

At LSPU-SC, we prioritize the security and privacy of your personal, academic, and inquiry-related information. The system is built with state-of-the-art security measures to protect user data from unauthorized access and to comply with applicable data protection laws and policies.

Confidential Communication

For offices like GAD and Guidance, WebRTC-based video calls ensure secure, private communication for sensitive inquiries such as counseling or gender-based concerns.

Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)

Access to data and features is restricted based on user roles. Only authorized personnel in each office can view or respond to specific inquiries.

Privacy Compliance

The system complies with data protection regulations such as the Data Privacy Act, ensuring that personal information is collected, stored, and processed with full user consent.

WebRTC

Real-time video and audio communications via WebRTC ensure private and direct peer-to-peer sessions between users, minimizing the risk of unauthorized access during interactions.

WebSocket

Real-time notifications via WebSockets are encrypted to prevent unauthorized data interception during communication.

Audit Trails

All actions performed within the system, including inquiry submissions, responses, and updates, are logged for accountability and transparency.

Our Commitment to Your Security

Data Protection Measures

  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Automatic session timeouts after periods of inactivity

User Privacy Rights

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to request data deletion
  • Control over your communication preferences
  • Transparent data usage policies

Questions About Security?

If you have any questions or concerns about how we handle your data, please don't hesitate to contact us. Your privacy and security are our top priorities.
