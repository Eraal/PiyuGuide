Your information is used solely to process your inquiries, provide support, and improve our services. We maintain detailed logs for security and quality assurance purposes.

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time. You can also request a copy of all data we have about you.

Security Tips for Students

Strong Passwords

Use unique, complex passwords and enable two-factor authentication when available.

Secure Connections

Always use secure networks and look for the lock icon in your browser when submitting forms.

Stay Vigilant

Be cautious of phishing attempts and never share your login credentials with others.

Device Security

Keep your devices updated and log out from public computers after use.
