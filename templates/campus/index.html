How does PiyuGuide work?

Experience seamless campus communication through our four core features

Real-Time Inquiry Responses

Stay informed with instant updates on your inquiries' status as they are processed by our dedicated team.

Office-specific Responses

Get responses tailored to your inquiry from the specific office you've contacted, ensuring quick and accurate support.

Secure Counseling

Request confidential counseling or schedule a secure video session with offices like Guidance and Counseling or GAD.

Announcements

Catch up on important news, updates, and announcements from various university units in real-time.
